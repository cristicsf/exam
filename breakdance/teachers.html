OUR TRAINERS

B-BOLD is an international dance school, therefore we focus on a vast majority of people who come from different places. To make you feel comfortable and to provide you with the best teachers we put the most skilled and famous breakdancers in the World together.


We are proud to present you the absolute ELITE of b-girls and b-boys in our Team.

Nathan

"If you believe it, you will achieve it."

Nathan has more than a decade of experience in b-boying. You might have seen him top rocking in Red Bull BC One Stars Crew. Being a part of the prestigious Crew is a huge honor and a tremendous dream of every dancer. Nathan is the right guy if you want to take your dance career seriously.

Roxy

"Take the risk or lose the chance."

Roxy is one of the most hardworking and determined dancers you could ever meet. She really pours her heart and her raw emotions into her dancing and that is why her style is so unique. No wonder she got qualified for the Olympics 2024 in Paris!

Frederico "Luigi" Lopez

"Make the roooom I gotta dance!"

Luigi is literally the walking sunshine in our crew but you would not want to challenge him in a dance battle. There is no battle that he has not won. He is the right person who will teach you how to compete and actually win. The strategy is important!

Dynamo

"It could be day one or one day, your choice."

Dynamo is the newest and youngest member of our team. Not only is she the best b-girl in Denmark, she gained her respect overseas as well. In fact she is the QUEEN of improvisation. It is unbelievable how easily she can charm you with her skills.
