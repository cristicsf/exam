OUR COURSES

BEGINNER

  • This course is for everyone who has never tried breaking before as well as for those who have only tried it at home. You will learn everything from the beginning from the best trainers who have lot of expierence. You can start this course every third month of the year from the first day of month.

  • Start of Course: Every 3rd month
  • Course Duration: Three months
  • Classes p.w.: Two
  • Class Duration: 70 minutes

ADVANCED

  • In this cours we welcome advanced breakers. If you already have some experiences and big passion for breakin´ this course will perfectly suit you. You will train hard with our trainers three times a week and in the end of the course you can sign in to the breakdance battle competition where you will be able to show your improvement.

  • Start of Course: October
  • Course Duration: Nine months
  • Classes p.w.: Three
  • Class Duration: 90 minutes

CHILDREN

  • In this course we will take children to the groups of their age. They will learn everything about breakin´from the beggining. After five months of this course we organize battle for children where they can show what they learned during this period. This battle is organized only to motivate children to train and we want them to have fun.

  • Start of Course: October
  • Course Duration: Nine months
  • Classes p.w.: Two
  • Class Duration: 50 minutes
